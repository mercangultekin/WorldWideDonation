Use Case Name: DisplayStartedProject
Participating Actors : Initiated by ProjectLeader
Communicates With WWDS
Entry Condition : ProjectLeader must logged-on to the system 
Flow of Events : 
1) ProjectLeader clicks to "My Account”. 
2) WWDS direct ProjectLeader to his accout page and in ProjectLeader's account page, ProjectLeader will see "My Account Settings", "My Projects" and "Reset Password" options.
3) ProjectLeader selects “My Projects” option.
4) WWDS direct ProjectLeader to his project page containing projects he has started in past. 
Exit Condition : ProjectLeader done viewing his project page and go other page.
